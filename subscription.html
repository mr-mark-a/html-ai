Frequently Asked Questions

How do I manage my subscription?
You can manage your subscription directly from your account settings. You'll receive a confirmation email with all your details and a link to manage or cancel anytime.
Can I change plans?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle. If upgrading, you'll receive a prorated credit.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay for maximum convenience and security.
Do unused tokens carry over?
No, tokens do not carry over between billing cycles. Each month you receive a fresh allotment. Use them wisely! Free users earn 20 tokens per day without expiration.
Is there a free trial?
We offer a 7-day free trial for new subscribers. Just verify your payment method—you won't be charged until the trial ends.
